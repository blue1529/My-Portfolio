Welcome to my personal portfolio website (newtonkalulu.vercel.app). This privacy policy explains how I collect, use, and protect information about visitors who interact with this site.

I value your privacy. This website is designed to minimize data collection and uses a privacy-friendly analytics system.

2. Information Collected

This website does not collect personally identifiable information (such as names, emails, or IP addresses). However, anonymous analytics data is collected through Umami Analytics to understand website usage patterns.

The following non-personal data may be collected:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page

This data is completely anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual visitors.

3. Use of Information

The analytics information collected is used solely to:

  • Monitor site performance and user engagement

4. Cookies

This website does not use cookies to track or store any information about you. The analytics platform (Umami) does not use cookies or store personal data.

5. Data Sharing

I do not sell, share, or rent any visitor data to third parties. All analytics data is stored securely and accessible only to me.

Analytics data is hosted securely on servers managed by Vercel and Neon Database through my Umami deployment. No raw IP addresses or personal identifiers are stored.
