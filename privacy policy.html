Privacy Policy & Data Protection

How I collect, use, and protect your information on my portfolio website

Welcome

This privacy policy explains how I handle information on my personal portfolio website (newtonkalulu.vercel.app). I value your privacy and designed this website to minimize data collection while providing transparency about any analytics used.

1

Information Collected

This website does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, emails, or IP addresses. I use Umami Analytics for privacy-friendly analytics that respect visitor privacy.

Non-personal data collected:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring websites (where visitors came from)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, or mobile)
  • Approximate region or country (no precise location)

Important: All data is completely anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual visitors. No cookies or personal identifiers are stored.

2

Use of Information

The analytics information collected is used solely for improving the website experience:

Performance Monitoring

Track site performance and user engagement

Content Improvement

Enhance content and design based on usage

Technical Maintenance

Detect and fix potential technical issues

3

Cookies & Tracking

No Cookies Used

This website does not use cookies to track or store any information about you. The analytics platform (Umami) does not use cookies or store personal data.

4

Data Sharing & Storage

No Data Sharing

I do not sell, share, or rent any visitor data to third parties. All analytics data is stored securely and accessible only to me.

Secure Data Storage

Analytics data is hosted securely on servers managed by Vercel and Neon Database through my Umami deployment. No raw IP addresses or personal identifiers are stored.

5

Your Rights & External Links

Your Privacy Rights

Because this site does not store personal data, there are no user accounts or identifiable records to modify or delete. However, if you have privacy concerns, I will respond promptly and transparently.

External Website Links

My portfolio may contain links to external websites (GitHub, LinkedIn, Formspree, etc.). I am not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external sites.

You are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

6

Policy Updates & Contact

Policy Updates

I may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in tools, hosting, or analytics services. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
